1.2a Padding

We downsample the spatial dimension by a factor of 2, with kernel size K = 4
and stride S = 2. We need to find the padding P.

The standard output-size formula for a 2D convolution is:
    Hout = (Hin - K + 2P) / S + 1

Since we downsample by a factor of 2, we require Hout = Hin / 2. Substituting
K = 4 and S = 2:

    Hin / 2 = (Hin - 4 + 2P) / 2 + 1
    Hin     = (Hin - 4 + 2P) + 2          (multiply both sides by 2)
    Hin     = Hin - 2 + 2P
    0       = -2 + 2P
    P       = 1

Sanity check with Hin = 64:
    Hout = (64 - 4 + 2*1) / 2 + 1 = 62 / 2 + 1 = 31 + 1 = 32   ✓

Therefore, the padding is P = 1.
